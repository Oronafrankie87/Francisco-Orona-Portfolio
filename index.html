These experiences have given me a creative edge in my work as a developer, and I approach each project with an open mind and fresh perspective. I have always been curious about how things work and from a very young age until now I've love taking things apart and putting them back together. I have strong programming skills, with expertise in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I am always looking for new challenges to enhance my skills and stay current with the latest trends and technologies in the industry. One of my greatest strengths is my ability to work well with others. I believe that collaboration and communication are key to achieving project goals, and I always strive to build strong relationships with clients and colleagues. In addition to my career as a developer, I also have a passion for music. My wife May and I play in a band together, and we recently released our first album. Music inspires my creative side and fuels my passion for exploring new ideas and possibilities. Overall, I am a dedicated and talented software developer with a diverse skill set and a passion for learning and growth. If you're looking for a skilled and collaborative developer who can bring a unique perspective to your team, look no further than Francisco Orona.
